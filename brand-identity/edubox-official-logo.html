Implementation Guidelines

  1. Clear Space: Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "E" around the logo
  2. Minimum Size: Don't use the full logo smaller than 40px height
  3. Background: Prefer white or light backgrounds; use reversed version on dark
  4. Don't alter: Don't stretch, rotate, or change colors
  5. Network elements: Can be animated for digital applications

This is the official EduBox logo. Consistent use across all touchpoints will build strong brand recognition.
