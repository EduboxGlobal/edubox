### Week 1: Logo Creation

#### Option A: Hire a Designer ($50-200)
1. **Fiverr Route** (Fastest, $50-150)
   - Search for "nonprofit logo design"
   - Look for designers with 4.8+ ratings
   - Check their portfolio for similar work
   - Send them our logo-design-brief.md

2. **99designs Contest** ($299+)
   - Get multiple concepts from different designers
   - Community votes on favorites
   - Guaranteed satisfaction

3. **Local Design Student** (Free-$100)
   - Contact local colleges with design programs
   - Post in university job boards
   - Great for portfolio building

#### Option B: DIY Approach (Free)
1. **Get Canva Nonprofit Account**
   - Apply at: canva.com/canva-for-nonprofits/
   - Free Pro access for registered nonprofits
   - Use their logo maker tool

2. **Design Process**
   - Start with simple shapes
   - Use our brand colors
   - Test at small sizes
   - Get team feedback

## üí° Quick Tips

### Logo Design
1. Start with black and white
2. Test at 16x16 pixels
3. Avoid thin lines
4. Keep it simple

### Color Usage
1. Use blue for trust/stability
2. Orange for CTAs only
3. Maintain high contrast
4. Test color blindness

### Typography
1. Use Open Sans for everything
2. Bold for headers
3. Regular for body
4. 16px minimum for web
